### About Myself 👋
Hi!
My name is Musa Demir. I love coding and developing. My software adventure started in high school computer classes. After a short break, I decided that I loved coding and must be computer engineer. I graduated the computer engineering department at Sakarya university. While studying at the university, I worked for more then two and half years in the IT department of my university which develops university management systems and sells them to many universities. After I graduated from university I have started to work in the Volosoft which develops [aspnetboilerplate](https://github.com/aspnetboilerplate/aspnetboilerplate) and [abp](https://github.com/abpframework/abp). and I continue to work fondly.

I mainly use C# and develop web applications and frameworks (thanks to aspnetboilerplate).  
I like open-source culture and follow open-source projects. I try to contribute open-source projects as much as I can, but not much enough.  
I also develop games using Unity.

This is a brief summary of my software life so far.
